Write an integrator¶

Purpose¶

An integrator is an extention of a ThingRunner. Their purpose is to interface with the source data on the “left” and provide events to the digital twin on the “right”. They may or may not need to poll the source systems, but that’s the most common way of turning an API into an event stream

+------------+            +------------+              +------------+
| Source     |<---Poll--->| Integrator |--->Events--->|   Twin     |
+------------+            +------------+              +------------+
